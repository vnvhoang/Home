Welcome to my personal website!

I am delighted to welcome you here and provide a glimpse into my journey as a young researcher with a profound passion for the field of computational mechanics. I earned my bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering from Hanoi University of Science and Technology in Hanoi, Vietnam. Currently, I am engaged in research as an international visiting scholar at Huanghuai University in Henan, China. Furthermore, I am on the brink of an exciting new chapter in my academic and professional life as I prepare to start a Master of Science program at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in Shanghai, China.

My ultimate aspiration is to become a leading scientist in the realm of computational mechanics. I warmly extend an invitation for you to join me on this thrilling journey of exploration, learning, and growth. Together, we will delve into the intricacies of mechanics, exchange our discoveries, and work towards a future where science and technology continuously advance for the betterment of our world. Your visit is greatly appreciated, and I eagerly anticipate the captivating journey that lies ahead.
