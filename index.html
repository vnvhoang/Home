Hello and welcome to my personal website!

My name is Vu Ngoc Viet Hoang, and I am a dedicated and passionate graduate student, focusing on computational mechanics. My drive stems from a profound fascination with understanding and manipulating the mechanical behavior of materials and structures. Throughout my academic journey, I have immersed myself in rigorous studies, practical research, and a relentless thirst for knowledge in the realm of mechanics.

My aspiration is to contribute significantly to the advancement of technology and industry by unraveling the complexities of solid mechanics, innovating in design methodologies, and pushing the boundaries of material performance. I am eager to collaborate, learn, and immerse myself in the vibrant community of professionals and scholars dedicated to making a lasting impact in this fascinating field.
