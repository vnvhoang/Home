I'm thrilled to share my research and contributions in computational mechanics, where I've explored the convergence of mathematics, engineering, and computer science to address intricate mechanical challenges. My work aims to provide creative solutions, deepen our understanding of structural and material behavior, and contribute to the dynamic field of computational mechanics. Dive into my research papers to uncover the exciting projects and discoveries that have shaped my academic journey. Thank you for visiting, and I hope my work both informs and inspires you.
